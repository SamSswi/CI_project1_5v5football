We want to make it possible for everyone in Groningen to be able to play football regularly


 Every year lots of people move to Groningen for studies or work. A big percentage of them are always excited about playing a friendly game of football. However, most of them simply don't have the necessary relationship network to be able to play football from time to time. And even if they do, often getting a group of 8-10 people to gather together for a game is difficult to organize and oftenly just an one time thing.


 Here at 5v5 Football Groningen we aim to help you overcome this obstacle and pair you up with people just as excited about football as you are. We only ask of you to be accepting and kind towards the others and to contribute paying for the venue renting. We split the amount of money equally between the people participating at the event and it is usually around 6-10 euros per person, depending on the amount of people gathered.
