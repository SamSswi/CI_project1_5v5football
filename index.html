How does it work?


1. Check out our events

Below we displayed the time and place of our football games

2. Choose one or more games

Choose what events we organize weekly suit you best. You don't need a team

3. Let us know about your choice

Within a few clicks join our community and you'll be able to participate in our football games

4. Show up for the game

Our members will be at the location 15 minutes prior to the event. Feel free to engage them

No obligations. Only pay for the games you signed up for

We only ask of you to contribute paying for the venue renting. We split the amount of money equally between the people participating at the event and it is usually around 6-10 euros per person, depending on the amount of people gathered.
